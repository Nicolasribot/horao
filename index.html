About

Horao is Oslandia's visualisation tool for 3D GIS data.

PostGIS 3D in Action from Oslandia on Vimeo.

Horao has two main components:

  • A simple viewer designed to listen to commands on its standard input. See doc/protocol.txt for the basic protocol specifications (still subject to changes)
  • A Python plugin that is used to connect QGIS signals to the viewer (in another process) to allow loading of QGIS layers with 3D geometries.
